About this Study


With the high growth in populations having access to these smart devices so too have we seen the explosive growthof mobile applications being made available through a variety of market places. As of 2017 there were approximately 2.7 million different applications available just on the Google play store alone. Breaking down theseapplications based on their classifications some of the most popular applications types fall into the following categories,Communication for example messaging applications and social media, Music for example music players and streamingmusic services such as Spotify, Medical and Health & Fitness which encompasses a wide range of applications fromhealth conditions management to calorie counters.

The focus of this paper is on these medical and health & fitness applications which fall broadly into the categoryof mHealth applications. FDA guidelines formalize use of mHealth apps for healthcare and recommendthose provide aid to patients or clinicians to be considered as standard medical devices.
